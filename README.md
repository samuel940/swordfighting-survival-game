# swordfighting-survival-game
https://samuel940.github.io/swordfighting-survival-game/ is the websight

Instructions: 
A and D to move left and right, Shift to sprint
W to jump
I to attack
O for specials (consumes energy):
O by itself: shoots a beam depending on the last direction you were moving that stops after 30 frames or when it hits an enemy
O + A or D: Dashing shash (instantly you left or right, killing any enemies you pass through

Do your best!
